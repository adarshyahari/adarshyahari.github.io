About

Ahoy! I'm Adarshya. A product designer committed to creatively aligning user needs and business goals.

At work, I'm passionate, ambitious, and innovative — always up for a challenge. I love solving problems while making headway on personally meaningful goals and going the extra mile.

Design is, by definition, uncertain. You're trying to give tangible form to a possible future so you can test it. You go into this knowing early iterations will be wrong — hopefully, usefully wrong. The whole point is to start a feedback loop that leads to something good.

In life, I'm a professional cat cuddler. On a Saturday night you'll find me at home watching a movie. I can never get a good drawing onto my sketchbook but I somehow end up doodling on every surface around me.
